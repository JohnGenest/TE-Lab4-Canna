THE IDEA

A website made to empower users to make informed decisions about their cannabis use through personalized recommendations based on their unique health needs and preferences, promoting wellness and mindful consumption.

THE TARGET AUDIENCE

  • Medical Cannabis Users: People seeking cannabis for relief from specific symptoms such as chronic pain, anxiety, insomnia, or PTSD.
  • Beginner Users: Those new to cannabis who want guidance on safe and appropriate strains based on their experience level.
  • Cannabis Enthusiasts: Users who are familiar with cannabis and looking for new strain recommendations tailored to their preferences or medical needs.

THE CONTENT

  • Text Content: Source strain descriptions and health benefits from reputable cannabis review sites, scientific journals, or directly from strain databases (such as Leafly or Weedmaps). Team could also write original content based on research.
  • Graphic Content: High-quality photos of strains, illustrations (which I have a large library of) explaining different cannabis effects, and possibly even user-generated content (e.g., reviews or testimonials).

SITE FLOW CHART

  • Landing Page: Introduction, mission statement, and clear CTA (call to action) to start the quiz.
  • Symptom Selection: Users can input or select specific medical conditions or symptoms they are experiencing (e.g., pain, anxiety, insomnia).
  • Strain Preferences: A section that asks users about their preferred effects (e.g., relaxation, focus) or their experience level with cannabis.
  • Results Page: After completing the quiz, users will see recommended cannabis strains based on their input, along with detailed info about each strain (effects, potency, etc.).
  • Optional: A product or dispensary locator based on strain availability in their area (if you want to partner with vendors).
